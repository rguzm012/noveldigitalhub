Privacy Policy – Simplify Notes

Effective Date: February 2026

Simplify Notes is a free note-taking application. We respect your privacy.

1. Data Collection

Simplify Notes does not collect, store, or share personal information directly.

  • The app does not require account registration.
  • All notes are stored locally on your device.

2. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data
  • Advertising ID (if enabled)

This information may be used to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

For more information, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (Google AdMob) that may collect information as described above.

4. User Control

You can limit ad tracking in your device settings:

Settings → Privacy → Tracking
